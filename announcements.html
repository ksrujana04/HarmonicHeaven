ANNOUNCEMENTS

Ganesh Visarjan

DATE: 28th Sept, 2023

As the sun dipped below the horizon, casting a warm golden glow over our community, residents gathered with beaming smiles and hearts full of devotion to bid farewell to Lord Ganesha during the grand Ganesh Visarjan celebrations.

Colorful Procession Through the Streets

The highlight of our Ganesh Visarjan celebration was the vibrant procession that wound its way through the streets of our locality. A beautifully adorned idol of Ganesha, lovingly brought into our homes at the beginning of the festival, was now being carried back to the Hussain Sagar Lake amidst the sound of drums. The idol adorned with flowers and traditional attire gleamed in the soft glow of the evening. Devotees, young and old, danced alongside, shouting "Ganapathi Bappa Morya" that filled the air with happiness and enthusiasm.

Ganesh Chaturthi Celebrations in Our Community

DATE: 18th Sept, 2023

It's a sunlit day with, alive with anticipation. The air is brimming with excitement and the people are adorning their homes with flowers and colorful rangolis. All the people of the community, both young and old gathered to celebrate the birth of Lord Ganesha, the remover of obstacles.

A Grand Inauguration

The festivities kicked off with the installation of the large Ganesha idol in the community. A huge pandal has been set up. Elaborate decorations, floral arrangements, and diyas adorned the idol creating an atmosphere of pure spirituality. The priest was called and after the completion of the pooja, prasad was distributed among the devotees. Dancing and singing programs were organized and gifts were given away to the winners. Lord Ganesha was worshipped for 9 days and on the 10th day, the Ganesha idol was immersed in the Hussain Sagar Lake.
