Driving in Rain? Be cautious

DATE: 25th July, 2023

With the state bracing for more rains in the coming days, motorists have been urged to exercise caution by the state government as the analysis of previous year shows that most of the fatal accidents occur during heavy rain. As we all know, the roads of our locality are in a very bad condition and water-logging makes it difficult to identify the potholes on the road, leading to a loss of control of the vehicle. So, please be careful while driving, especially during the night. Yesterday, there was an accident near the XYZ junction. Due to the heavy rains, the roads have become slippery and a car lost control and crashed into a nearby tree. Luckily, all the passengers were safe. So please be cautious while driving and make sure that you drive at a speed 20% less than your original speed. Also make sure that all the manholes are closed. Please follow the rules to ensure the safety of all the people in the locality.
